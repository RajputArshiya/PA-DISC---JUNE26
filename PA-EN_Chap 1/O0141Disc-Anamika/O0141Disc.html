The product 4 × 4 × 4 × 4 × 4 is written as 45, and is read the fifth power of 4, or 4 to the fifth power.
45 =
4 × 4 × 4 × 4 × 4
= 1,024.
In 45, 4 is the base, and 5 is called the exponent or power.

To evaluate a number written in exponential form, the base is used as a factor as many times as the exponent. Observe that 1n = 1 for all n.
Rule 1 :
If the exponent is 1, the value is same as the base.
(– 3)1 = – 3,
(– 8)1 = – 8,
(– 150)1 = – 150,
Rule 2 :
If the exponent is not 1, use the base as a factor as many times as the exponent and compute the product.
(– 4)3 = (– 4) (– 4) (– 4)
= (– 4)3    =    – 64
(– 3)6 = (– 3) (– 3) (– 3) (– 3) (– 3) (– 3)
= 36    =    729
Observe that, when the base is a negative number, the value of the exponential expression is negative or positive depending on whether the exponent is an even numner or odd number.

To find the value of a number written in exponential form:  24
Step 1: Identify the base and the exponent.
The base is 2 and the exponent is 4.
Step 2: Use the base as a factor as many times as the exponent of the base.
24   =    2 · 2 · 2 · 2
       =    4 · 4
       =    16
